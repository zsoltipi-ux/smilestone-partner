What Can You Expect to Earn?

Commission structure is simple. Here's what it looks like:

How It Works

  • One-time implementation fee: you set the price, you invoice the lab directly
  • Recurring commission: 20% of the lab's monthly subscription (as long as they stay with us)
  • Premium modules, extra users, add-ons: all count toward commission
  • Payments settle monthly; we send a statement, you invoice, payment within 15 days

Contract termination? You keep earning commission from customers you brought for 12 months after.

Partner Agreement — Outline

This is a simplified summary, not a final legal document. Exact terms are discussed personally.

PARTNER AGREEMENT

DRAFT
1. Partnership Subject and Scope
  • Partner is authorized to act on behalf of SmileStone for sales, marketing, and implementation purposes, in the 5–50 person SMB segment
  • Partner may use the SmileStone logo, brand name, and marketing materials according to brand guidelines
  • Partner is not authorized to sign contracts on behalf of SmileStone, modify payment terms, or make development promises
2. Lead Management and Regional Cooperation
  • Every potential customer must be reported to SmileStone within 24 hours
  • Upon valid registration, the lead belongs to the Partner for 90 days
  • SmileStone may also pass along inbound leads from the region — these come with full commission rights
  • Non-circumvention: neither party bypasses the other for commission purposes
3. Implementation and Pricing
  • Partner freely sets the price for implementation/professional services, and invoices the customer directly
  • The customer pays SmileStone's software subscription fee directly to SmileStone
4. Commission and Settlement
  • Monthly commission: 20% of the customer's net subscription fee, for as long as they remain an active subscriber
  • Premium packages, extra modules, additional users — all count toward the commission base
  • Settlement: SmileStone sends a monthly statement, Partner invoices, payment within 15 days
  • After contract termination, the Partner continues to earn commission from previously brought customers for 12 months
5. Support and Technical Cooperation
  • Partner provides 1st-line support (user questions, basic troubleshooting)
  • SmileStone provides 2nd-line support (bugs, configuration, performance issues)
  • L2 escalation: response within 4 hours during business hours
6. Onboarding and Training
  • SmileStone handles product configuration and customization
  • Partner is responsible for day-to-day lab training and guidance
  • Quarterly online training, continuously updated knowledge base
7. Brand Use and Marketing
  • Partner may use materials provided by SmileStone; custom materials require prior approval
  • SmileStone may list Partner as a reference
8. Data Protection and GDPR
  • Both parties comply with GDPR and local data protection regulations
  • Personal data may only be processed to the extent necessary to fulfill the contract
9. Liability and Indemnification
  • SmileStone is responsible for software availability and bug fixes
  • Partner is responsible for the legality of their own sales promises and services
  • Liability cap: maximum of 12 months of commission fees
10. Contract Termination
  • Indefinite term, either party may terminate with 30 days' notice
  • Immediate termination in case of material breach, insolvency, or illegal activity
  • After termination, these remain in effect: commission rights, confidentiality, data protection
11. Other Terms
  • Confidentiality: contract and business information must be handled confidentially
  • No exclusivity — multiple partners can operate in parallel
  • Applicable law: Hungarian law, disputes: Budapest Court
⚠️ This is an informational draft. The final contract will be prepared by legal counsel, and details are discussed personally after application.
